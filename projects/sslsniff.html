SSL Sniff Research

A research done during Summer 2017. We worked on the tool developed by Moxie Marlinspike, SSL Sniff, learning on how the tool used the vulnerability that existed in older version of browsers. We planned to make an activity that could be done in classes for students to get a better understanding of the tool.

Doing the research, we learned on topics such as SSL, Certificates, and partially used ARP Spoofing. We couldn't get the Targeted mode to work, but we were able to get the Authority Mode to work.
