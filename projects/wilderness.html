Wilderness

Participated in the Text-based Adventure Game "Wilderness" developed in the Student Game Developers Club in UVA during the Fall 2016 Semester. Join the minimalist text-based adventure of Kipp and Lore, each from different ends of the planet.

My contribution to the project is mostly included in script parsing and packaging of the game into executables.
