Dead Life of Samantha Yarn

First Game developed with Unity in the Computer Game Design course (CS4730) in University of Virginia during Fall of 2017. Its a puzzle adventure game inspird by Zelda, Pokemon and Earthbound.

The story follows the player who's sister is sick, and asks to solve the puzzles in the town while before its too late.

  • Language: C#, Unity
  • Year: 2017 Fall
