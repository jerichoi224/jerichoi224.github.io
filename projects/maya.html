Autodesk Maya

Took the course ARCH 5422 [Computer Animation: Design in Motion] where I learned basics techniques for animating in Maya including Keyframing, texture maps, rigging, special effects and more.

Below are the projects/exercises I've done throughout the course, and hopefully I get time to make some more in my free time.

  • Course: ARCH 5422
  • Program: Autodesk Maya 2016
  • Year: 2017 Summer
Exercise 1: short animation of a process

Create an animation of up to 30 seconds using geometrically abstract objects in motion and with a short thematic idea.

Exercise 2: in and out of time

Create a 1 to 2 minute animation with human, machine-like and/or anthropomorphic form that incorporates varied passages of time.

Final Animation

Create a roughly 1 to 3 minute animation incorporating any of the techniques and areas of emphasis (process, time, light)
we have covered this term into a short story sequence on a theme or subject of your own choosing.
